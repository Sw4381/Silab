Research Focus

XAI (Explainable AI), Graph AI and Security

He is focused on AI-driven security research. Over the years, he has conducted numerous studies on AI-based malware detection, network security, and endpoint security. Recently, his research has expanded to include explainable AI for trustworthy AI, graph AI for security problems with complex topology information, and security log analysis using large language models.
