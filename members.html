Academic Background & Experience

Taejin Lee is currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Information Security at Gachon University (2025–present). Prior to this, he served as an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Engineering at Hoseo University (2017–2024). Before transitioning to academia, he worked at the Korea Internet & Security Agency (KISA) as an R&D Team Leader (2003–2017), where he was involved in cybersecurity research and development. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Ajou University, his M.S. in Computer Engineering from Yonsei University, and his B.S. in Computer Engineering from POSTECH.

Research Focus

XAI (Explainable AI), Graph AI and Security

He is focused on AI-driven security research. Over the years, he has conducted numerous studies on AI-based malware detection, network security, and endpoint security. Recently, his research has expanded to include explainable AI for trustworthy AI, graph AI for security problems with complex topology information, and security log analysis using large language models.
